Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 July 2025  •  Compliant with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA)
We do not sell your personal data. We collect only what is necessary to operate the Service.

This Privacy Policy explains how Ideal One Technology ("Company", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects personal data in connection with IdealOMS ("Service"). It applies to all users including merchants, logistics operators, and end-customer data submitted through the platform.

1. Data We Collect

Account data: your name, business name, email address, and login credentials when you register for the Service.

Order data: order IDs, product details, recipient names, shipping addresses, order values, and fulfilment status imported from connected marketplaces or entered manually.

Marketplace credentials: OAuth access tokens obtained when you connect Shopify, TikTok Shop, Lazada, Shopee, or other platforms. These are stored encrypted and used solely to retrieve your order data.

Usage data: log entries including IP address, timestamps, API call patterns, and error logs for operational and security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and operate the Service, including syncing marketplace orders
  • To authenticate your identity and maintain session security
  • To send you transactional notifications (e.g. sync errors, account changes)
  • To investigate security incidents and prevent fraud
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations in Singapore

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to operating the Service.

3. End-Customer Data

Order data you import into the Service may contain personal data of your end customers (e.g. recipient names and delivery addresses). You are the data controller for that data; we act as your data processor. You are responsible for ensuring you have the right to transfer that data to us in accordance with applicable laws, including the PDPA.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers located in Singapore or within the Asia-Pacific region. We apply industry-standard security measures including encrypted storage of credentials, HTTPS for all data in transit, and access controls limiting who can view your data.

Staff access to production data is logged and restricted to authorised personnel for support and maintenance purposes only.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Infrastructure providers (cloud hosting, database services) under confidentiality obligations
  • Third-party marketplaces only to the extent necessary to authenticate and retrieve your order data
  • Law enforcement or regulators if required by Singapore law or a valid legal order

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and order data for as long as your account is active. If you close your account, your data remains available for export for 30 days, after which it is deleted from our systems. Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days before automatic deletion.

7. Your Rights Under the PDPA

Under Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act 2012, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Withdraw consent and request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Be informed of the purposes for which your data is being collected

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at hello@idealone.tech. We will respond within 10 business days.

8. Cookies

The Service uses session storage in your browser to maintain your login state. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

9. Third-Party Links

The Service may contain links to third-party platforms (Shopify, TikTok Shop, Lazada, Shopee). Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies and we are not responsible for them.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for business use and is not directed at individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or an in-app notice at least 14 days before taking effect. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
