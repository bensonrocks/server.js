The Problem

Manual picking is costing you more than you think

Every wrong item packed costs time, money, and client trust. Spreadsheets and tally marks are not a system — they are a liability.

😓 The Old Way

  • Staff manually tally counts on paper or Excel
  • Wrong items packed — only caught at delivery
  • No live visibility into scan progress
  • Re-keying data into WMS is slow and error-prone
  • Waybill PDFs managed manually in separate folders
  • No timestamp or operator record per order
  • Disputes with clients have no supporting data

✅ With IDEALONE

  • Every scan is verified against the required quantity in real time
  • Cannot complete an order until all items are confirmed
  • Live progress visible across all orders in the batch
  • WMS-format XLSX generated instantly — no re-keying
  • Carrier waybill PDFs auto-split and matched per order
  • Every order stamped with operator ID, start time, and end time
  • Full exportable audit trail proves exactly what was packed and when
Features

Everything your warehouse needs, nothing it doesn't

Purpose-built for 3PL and fulfilment operations. No bloat, no training days, no per-scan fees.

📡

Real-Time Barcode Scanning

Plug in any USB barcode scanner or use the built-in phone camera mode. Each scan is verified instantly — wrong items are rejected before they reach the box.

📄

Universal Picklist Import

Upload any XLSX, XLS, or CSV picklist. IDEALONE auto-detects column names regardless of layout — no template matching or pre-formatting required from your clients.

🏭

Instant WMS Export

Every uploaded batch is converted to your WMS column format automatically. Download the file directly from the admin panel — ready to import, no editing needed.

📦

Carrier Waybill PDF Matching

Upload the carrier's multi-page waybill PDF. IDEALONE splits it and matches each page to the correct order by waybill number or order number — automatically.

📊

Full Audit Trail

Every order records the operator who scanned it, when they started, when they finished, and the exact quantities picked. Export as XLSX for client reporting or dispute resolution.

👥

Multi-User with Role Control

Create separate accounts for each warehouse operator and administrator. Each user connects to their own printer with their own label size. One active session per account — always.

✉️

Automatic Email Alerts

IDEALONE sends an email notification the moment a warehouse operator marks an order complete. Keep your ops team informed without needing to check the dashboard.

🏷️

IDEALONE Label Printing

Generate a branded barcode shipping label for any completed order — including order number, customer name, carrier, and a CODE128 barcode. Supports 100×160 mm, 100×150 mm, and 4×6 in label stock.

📱

Works on Any Device

Browser-based — no installation. Works on desktop with a handheld scanner, tablet on the warehouse floor, or phone in the field. No app store, no updates to manage.

How It Works

From picklist to dispatch in three steps

A shift that used to take two people and a spreadsheet now runs on one screen.

1

Upload the Picklist

Drag and drop your client's XLSX or CSV file. Optionally attach the carrier's waybill PDF. IDEALONE creates the order batch in seconds.

  • Any column layout — auto-detected
  • Up to 5,000 rows per file
  • Waybill PDF split & matched automatically
2

Scan Every Item

Select an order and start scanning barcodes. Each scan is verified against the required quantity. The overlay shows live progress per item line.

  • USB scanner or phone camera
  • Over-scan & wrong-item alerts
  • Pause and resume at any time
3

Export & Dispatch

Mark complete. Download the matched waybill page for that order. The WMS export file and full audit trail are available in the Admin panel.

  • WMS-ready XLSX — no re-keying
  • Per-order waybill PDF download
  • Operator & timestamp on every record
