What exactly is ICT / Smart Money Concepts?

ICT (Inner Circle Trader) is a methodology that tracks the footprints of institutional traders — banks, hedge funds, central banks — through order blocks, fair value gaps, and market structure. Rather than following retail indicators like MACD or RSI, SMC identifies where "smart money" is positioned and trades alongside them.
