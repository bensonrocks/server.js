Fulfillment Scanning System

Replacing paper-based pick verification with a live, real-time scan workflow — from order upload to printed shipping label, all in one place.

📄 Download Overview Document

Why This Exists

1
No single source of truth

Order data arrives by email, spreadsheet, and WhatsApp — not in one system staff can act on.

2
Manual verification fails at scale

Counting by hand and ticking paper tally sheets is slow, inconsistent, and doesn't scale.

3
No audit trail

When a wrong item ships or a quantity is disputed, there's no record of who verified what and when.

4
Labels require a separate system

Producing shipping labels means switching apps, copying order details manually, and risking errors.

What You Can Do

📤
Upload order files
CSV or XLSX from any client — auto-mapped to WMS format instantly.
🔍
Scan & verify orders
Live barcode scan with instant pass/fail per item and per order.
📦
Scan waybill to jump to order
Point a barcode gun at any parcel — lands on that order's scan screen.
📍
See full delivery details
Address, phone, and platform shown during scanning for every order.
🖨
Print waybill labels
One click after verification — carrier, buyer, items, and barcode on one label.
📊
Export audit reports
Full XLSX export: who scanned what, when, how long, and whether it was correct.
