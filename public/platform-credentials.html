💡 How It Works

  • ZORT: Unified connector to Shopee, Lazada, TikTok, Shopify. One credential, all platforms.
  • Direct APIs: Connect directly to each platform without ZORT. Full control over your keys.
  • Future Migration: Add direct API credentials anytime. Switch platforms without losing data.
  • Credential Safety: All credentials encrypted. Soft-delete support for safe rollback.
