How it works

Three steps, same as the floor already runs.

STEP 1

Upload the order file

XLSX, CSV, or a photo of a printed picking list — column names are detected automatically, whatever the client's format.

STEP 2

Scan it off the floor

Pickers and receivers work from one scan screen. Progress, cartons, and exceptions update live for everyone watching.

STEP 3

Ship it, and keep the record

Every completed order leaves a slip, a carton breakdown, and an audit trail — searchable months later, even after the upload is gone.
