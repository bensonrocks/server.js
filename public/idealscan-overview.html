Why IDEALSCAN Exists

The Problem We Solve

Warehouses receiving orders from multiple e-commerce platforms face a fragmented workflow: reformatting client files, printing picklists, manually counting items, ticking tally sheets, and printing labels — all separately, all prone to human error, all leaving no audit trail.
1

No single source of truth. Order data lives in email attachments, spreadsheets, and WhatsApp messages — not in one system staff can act on.

2

Manual verification fails at scale. Counting items by hand and ticking paper sheets is slow, inconsistent, and doesn't scale beyond a handful of orders per hour.

3

No audit trail. When a wrong item ships or a quantity mismatch is disputed, there is no record of who scanned what, when, and whether it was correct.

4

Label printing is a separate step. Producing waybill labels requires switching to another system, copying order details manually, and hoping nothing is copied wrong.

What IDEALSCAN Does

Core Capabilities

📤
Order Upload & Auto-Mapping

Upload any client order file (CSV or XLSX). The system auto-detects column headers regardless of naming convention and converts the data into WMS-format output in seconds.

manual reformatting per batch
🔍
Live Scan Verification

Staff scan each item barcode on screen. Quantities are tracked in real time with an instant verdict — READY TO SHIP or NEEDS REVIEW — so errors are caught before the parcel leaves.

paper tally sheets and counting errors
📦
Waybill Scan Jump

Point a barcode gun at the shipping label on any parcel and the system jumps directly to that order's scan screen. No scrolling, no searching.

30–60 seconds per order × hundreds of orders per day
📍
Full Delivery Info On-Screen

Buyer name, complete delivery address, phone number, and platform channel are shown during scanning so staff can verify the right parcel goes to the right person.

wrong-address shipments and customer complaints
🖨
Waybill Label Printing

After a verified order, print a shipping label with carrier header, buyer details, item list, quantities, platform badge, and a CODE128 barcode of the waybill number — sized for any label printer.

manual label design and cross-system switching
🖨
Per-User Printer Profiles

Each staff member configures their own printer name and label size (100×160 mm, 100×150 mm, 4"×6"). Print jobs route to the correct printer for each workstation automatically.

mis-routed print jobs and reprinting waste
📊
Audit Trail & Reporting

Every scan session is recorded: who scanned, which order, how long it took, scan start and end times, and whether the result was correct. Exportable as XLSX at any time.

manual logging and compliance preparation
⚙️
Admin & User Management

Admins manage users, configure WMS output templates, set up email notifications, view upload history, and export reports — all from a password-protected admin panel.

ad-hoc admin tasks and training errors
